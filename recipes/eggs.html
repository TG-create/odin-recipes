Scrambled Eggs with Onions and Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • Five Eggs
  • One Yellow Onion
  • Two Tomatoes
  • Two Green Peppers
  • Olive Oil/Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Chop Onions and Tomatoes into small pieces. Cut up green pepper
  2. Add Onions to pan and saute for a bit then add Oil. After 2 minuites add tomatoes and let it mix for 3 minuites.
  3. Mix eggs in a bowl then add to the pan. Mix egg with the Onion and tomatoes and cook until it's almost done and add green peppers. Let Green peppers mix for a bit and take the pan off heat.
  4. Done! Serve with toasted Bread and coffee/tea
