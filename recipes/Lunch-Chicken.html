Deli Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Sliced Deli Chicken
  • Provolone Cheese(or any other cheese)
  • Sliced bread
  • Cut lettuce
  • Spicy Ranch

Steps

  1. Toast 2 bread slices
  2. I like to warm up my deli chicken to be safe. You can skip this step if you prefer it cold
  3. spread spicy ranch on one side of bread, add chicken on top, more ranch,then top with cheese, more ranch, lettuce, more ranch and put the other sliced bread on top and Done.
  4. Serve
